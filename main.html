What is world hunger???

World hunger is a current global issue that is happening in areas that is experiencing poverty.

The Statistics behind world hunger!!!

Around 733 million people faced hunger in 2023, equivalent to one in eleven people globally and one in five in Africa, according to the latest State of Food Security and Nutrition in the World (SOFI) report published today by five United Nations specialized agencies. More people in the poor side of Africa is expereincing world hunger. This is because there are government bodies that is corrupt. Instead of investing for their people, they invest for their riches.

How can we solve world hunger???

1. Food and cash vouchers during emergencies, keeping mouths fed while families recover.

2. Increasing water supplies for crops as well as for cooking nutritious food.

3. Training sustainable farming practices to improve food security and bring in additional incomes.
